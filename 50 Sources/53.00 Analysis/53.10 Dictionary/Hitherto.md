## ~={purple}Technical=~
##### ~={lightpurple}Meaning:=~ 
- Until this time.
- To this place; to a prescribed limit.

##### ~={lightpurple}Part of speech:=~
adverb

##### ~={lightpurple}Pronunciation:=~
/hĭᴛʜ′ər-too͞″, hĭᴛʜ″ər-too͞′/

##### ~={lightpurple}Related words:=~
- Up to this time
- As yet
- Until now

##### ~={lightpurple}Note:=~  
It is often used to refer to something that has been true or the case up to the present moment.
