# ~={wine}Zero Conditional=~

We use the zero conditional to talk about a specific, something that is generally true.
- Condition (if-clause): present simple
- Result (main clause): present simple
- Example: If you die, you are dead.
# ~={wine}First Conditional=~

We use the first conditional to talk about possible situations and their consequences.
- Condition (if-clause): present simple
- Result (main clause): will +infinitive
- Example: If you die, I won't cry bitch.

# ~={wine}Second Conditional=~
We use the second conditional to talk about things in the future that are probable, improbable and imaginary.
- Condition (if-clause): past simple
- Result (main clause): would +infinitive
- Example: If you died, I would chill at your grave for a few days to help you meet new people.
